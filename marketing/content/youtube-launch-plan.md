### FILMING NOTES (phone, $0 budget)

- Film in a room with a window in front of you, not behind you. Natural light is free
  and it works.
- Prop your phone against a stack of books or a mug — you do not need a tripod.
- Record the app screen separately using your phone's built-in screen recorder,
  then cut the clips together in a free editor (CapCut works on mobile, iMovie
  on Mac, DaVinci Resolve free on desktop).
- You do not need to re-film if you stumble. Pause, breathe, pick up from the last
  sentence. Cut in editing.
- Speak to one person. Not "you guys." Just "you." It feels more personal on camera
  and it is.
- The script above is a guide, not a teleprompter. Know the sections. Talk it naturally.
  Your real voice is better than a read voice.

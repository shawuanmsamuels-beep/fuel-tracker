## Quick Tips for Publishing

- Pin 1–2 pins per day rather than all at once — Pinterest rewards consistent activity.
- Use the exact Pin Title text as the on-platform title, not just the image text.
- Add the destination URL in the pin's link field, not just the description.
- Repin your own content to a second relevant board 2–3 weeks later (it refreshes reach).
- Pinterest boards take 2–4 weeks to begin surfacing in search — post consistently and be patient.

## The product (facts — keep these accurate)
- **Name:** Fuel Tracker
- **What it is:** a simple, bright calorie & macro tracking web app.
- **Live at:** https://fueltracker-app.netlify.app  (custom domain coming)
- **Core features:** search 3M+ real foods (incl. restaurants), personalized
  calorie & macro goals, weight tracking with a progress chart, auto-save +
  cloud sync across devices, works in any browser (no app store needed).
- **Offer:** 7-day FREE trial, **no credit card required**. Then $9.99/month
  or $79/year (save 34%). 30-day money-back guarantee. Cancel anytime.

## Who it's for (broad — speak to all three)
1. **Weight-loss beginners** — want simple, encouraging tracking, not overwhelm.
2. **Fitness / macro crowd** — care about protein/macros and accuracy.
3. **Busy parents & professionals** — need fast, no-fuss tracking that fits a hectic life.

## Brand voice (how every post should sound)
- **Encouraging, never preachy.** We're a supportive coach, not a drill sergeant.
- **Plain-spoken.** No jargon. A beginner should understand every word.
- **Honest & human.** Built solo by a real founder — lean into that authenticity.
- **Motivating, with a light touch.** Warmth + the occasional bit of humor.
- **Action-first.** Every post should make tracking feel *easy* and *doable today*.
- Recurring idea/tagline material: **"Fuel your goals."** / "Small logs, big results."

### Do
- Lead with the reader's problem or win ("Lost on calories? Start here.").
- Use short lines, real examples, concrete numbers (3M+ foods, 7 days free).
- Always make the next step obvious (start the free trial — no card).

### Don't
- Don't promise medical results or use shame/fear ("you're failing").
- Don't use heavy fitness jargon or guru-speak.
- Don't invent fake stats, testimonials, or features we don't have.
- Don't sound like a faceless corporation.

## Why us (differentiators to lean on)
- **Free to start, no card** — lowest-risk way to try.
- **3M+ foods** including restaurant meals — find what you actually eat.
- **Auto-save + cloud sync** — never lose your data; pick up on any device.
- **Bright, simple design** — tracking that doesn't feel like a chore.

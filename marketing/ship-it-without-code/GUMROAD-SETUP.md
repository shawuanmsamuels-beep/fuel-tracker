# Selling "Ship It Without Code" — Gumroad setup (≈20 min, free)

Gumroad is the easiest way to sell a digital file: it takes the payment AND
delivers the download automatically. No code, free to start (they take a small
cut per sale). Works today even while your Stripe is locked.

## Step 1 — Create the product
1. Sign up at **gumroad.com** (free).
2. Click **New product → Digital product**.
3. **Name:** `Ship It Without Code — 50 AI Prompts to Build & Launch Your First Paid Product`
4. **Price:** `19.99`
5. **Upload the file:** your kit as a **PDF** (a PDF is what buyers expect — ask me and I'll
   turn your Word doc into a clean PDF). You can also include the Word version as a bonus.
6. Publish. Gumroad gives you a **product URL** (looks like `https://yourname.gumroad.com/l/shipit`).

## Step 2 — Connect it to your sales page
1. Open `marketing/ship-it-without-code/index.html`.
2. Find-and-replace every **`BUY_LINK`** with your real Gumroad URL (there are 4).
3. Host the page free: drag the file onto **app.netlify.com/drop**, or add it as a page on
   **shawuanwrites.com**. (I can also wire it up for you.)

> Tip: Gumroad also has an "Overlay" buy button — but a simple link to your product URL is
> the easiest and works perfectly.

In Gumroad settings, turn on **Refunds** (or just honor the 30-day guarantee manually).
